PROJECT IDEA: FarmLink – Connecting Farmers and Buyers
1. PROBLEM DEFINITION

(No technical words)

Who is your user?

Small-scale farmers in rural areas

Food traders and buyers in cities

What problem do they face?

Farmers struggle to find reliable buyers for their products.

Buyers do not always know where to find fresh food at a fair price.

Why does it matter?

Farmers lose money because they sell their products cheaply.

Food becomes expensive in cities.

A lot of farm produce goes to waste before reaching the market.

2. REQUIREMENT DRAFT
3 Must-Have Features

Farmers can post the products they want to sell.

Buyers can view available products near them.

Buyers can order products directly from farmers.

2 Non-Functional Requirements

The platform should work well even with slow internet.

The system must be simple and easy for anyone to use.

3. ONE-MONTH ROADMAP
3 Clear Milestones

Week 1
Design the idea and plan how the platform will work.

Week 2
Build a simple prototype where farmers can post their products.

Week 3–4
Add the ability for buyers to place orders.

3 Measurable Goals

20 farmers sign up and list their products.

50 buyers use the platform.

At least 100 product listings are posted.

1 User Validation

Test the platform with 5 real farmers and collect their feedback to improve the system.

4. GO-TO-MARKET PLAN
Who are your first 100 users?

Small farmers

Local market traders

Where will you find them?

Local markets

Farmer cooperatives

WhatsApp groups for farmers

Adoption Goals in 90 Days

100 farmers using the platform

300 buyers registered

At least 500 transactions
